Daily Devotional May 16, 2026

Anchored in the Eternal

"Set your minds on things above, not on earthly things."

— Colossians 3:2

Heaven is not merely a destination — it is a dimension of reality accessible to you right now, through faith, prayer, and the living word of God. The enemy's greatest strategy is to keep your gaze anchored to the temporal: your circumstances, your limitations, your lack. But God invites you daily into a higher vantage point. Today, make a deliberate act of setting your mind above. Let prayer be the ladder. Let worship be the atmosphere. Watch as he transforms your perspective from the temporary to the eternal, from anxiety to authority, from striving to rest.
